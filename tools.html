Tools

Bloomsbury offers free online tools to help New Zealanders think clearly about money and giving. Each tool is illustrative only — not personal financial advice. If you would like tailored guidance, book a free initial consultation.

Philanthropic giving — three connected tools

For donors who want to give with confidence, we have built three connected tools: understand what you can afford to give, find charities that fit your values, then shape a portfolio of gifts you can track over time.

Step 1

The Giving Gauge

Enter a few details about your situation and see an indicative sustainable annual donation — with a projection of how giving might look through retirement. Takes around five minutes.

Step 2

Charity Scout Coming soon

Search the New Zealand Charities Register by name, purpose, and where charities operate. Compare organisations and bookmark the ones you want to explore further.

Step 3

Donor Portfolio Coming soon

Build a charitable portfolio with dollar amounts on each cause, then see allocation-style charts by charity, sector, and New Zealand vs overseas focus.

These tools use assumptions you enter and public register data. Results are for education and planning conversations only. They do not replace advice that takes account of your full circumstances.

BrokerCheck

For investors with a sharebroker, BrokerCheck is a free portfolio review from Bloomsbury’s accredited fiduciaries. Upload a recent broker statement and we will send you a plain-English report on fees, diversification, tax structure, and any conflicts of interest worth knowing about.
