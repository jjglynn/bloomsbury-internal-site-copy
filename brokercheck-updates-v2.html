BrokerCheck

Bloomsbury’s BrokerCheck is a free portfolio analysis report for brokerage clients, prepared for you by Accredited Investment Fiduciaries (AIF©)

Upload your broker statement and we'll go through it properly – fees, performance, diversification, tax structure, and any signs that the portfolio has been built around the broker's interests rather than yours. No appointment needed, no obligation [to act], and no charge.

  • Reviewed by qualified, experienced advisers at a CEFEX-certified advisory firm.
  • Bloomsbury Associates are an independent adviser - we don’t take commissions or earn money from trading.
  • BrokerCheck is a free, detailed analysis of your broker portfolio.
  • No obligation to act on the report now or in the future.
  • Completely confidential.

Request a BrokerCheck report now

How It Works

Bloomsbury’s BrokerCheck is straightforward. No meetings, no lengthy questionnaires. All we need is your portfolio statement. Our qualified advisers and accredited investment fiduciaries will take care of the rest.

1 Upload your portfolio statement

No in-person appointment needed. Just provide a recent PDF statement from your broker. We’ll need your name, and for you to sign a brief scope of engagement.

2 Thorough review by Bloomsbury advisers

Our advisers study your portfolio with fresh eyes. Subject to the information you upload, we'll look for conflicts of interest, analyse your portfolio's asset allocation, consider your portfolio's tax efficiency, evaluate your historical returns, and investigate the costs and fees.

3 You get a straight answer

We email you a clear, plain-English report with our findings. We’ll tell you what’s working, what isn’t, and what a better structure might look like.

Get Your Free BrokerCheck Report

Enter your details and attach your most recent broker statement (PDF). We'll come back to you with an honest assessment of what we find — fees, performance, diversification, tax structure, and any conflicts of interest worth knowing about.


Free · Confidential · No obligation

Bloomsbury Associates reserves the right to refrain from providing a BrokerCheck report. If a BrokerCheck report is provided, we endeavour to produce it in 2-3 weeks, however reports may take longer based on availability.
