Research

Bloomsbury's research goes beyond market commentary. We publish longer-form work when a topic deserves depth — especially where wealth, governance, and giving intersect with New Zealand's future.

Our white papers draw on interviews, data, and our experience advising families and donors. They are written for trustees, advisers, charity leaders, and anyone who wants evidence-based discussion, not slogans.

We are preparing a second white paper for release in 2026. If you would like to be notified when it is published, subscribe via the newsletter sign-up in the page footer.

The future of charitable giving in New Zealand

This white paper was written to ignite thoughtful discussion and bold thinking about how New Zealand can foster a stronger culture of giving — one that empowers donors, strengthens charities, and delivers greater public good. Read more about philanthropy in New Zealand
